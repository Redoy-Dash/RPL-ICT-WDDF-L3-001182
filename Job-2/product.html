Monstera Growth Stages & Care Routine

Watch the video to learn how to care for your Monstera plant from seedling to maturity!

Monstera Care Tips

  • Watering: Water thoroughly when the top 1-2 inches of soil are dry.
  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light.
  • Humidity: Keep humidity levels high to support healthy growth.
  • Fertilization: Fertilize once a month during the growing season.

Growth Stages

  • Stage 1: Seedling with simple leaves.
  • Stage 2: New leaves start developing holes (fenestration).
  • Stage 3: Mature Monstera with large, split leaves.

Plant Care Information

Plant Size Options Light Requirements Watering Requirements Toxicity Level Repotting Recommendations
Small (Up to 12 inches) Low to Medium light Water when soil is dry Non-toxic Repot every 1-2 years
Medium (12-24 inches) Bright, indirect light Keep soil moist but not soggy Mildly toxic to pets Repot every 2-3 years
Large (24+ inches) Full sunlight Water frequently Highly toxic to pets Repot every 3-4 years
